Data Description
This dataset contains shapefiles representing Great Lakes ice concentration. Each shapefile includes spatial data where the attribute values correspond to the water or ice concentration across the Great Lakes. The shapefiles can be opened by ArcGIS, QGIS, or Python (e.g. geopandas library). 
Value Definitions:
0             --- Water
5–100   --- Ice concentration (in percent)

Data Source
This product was originally produced by the National Ice Center (NIC) and further developed by the NOAA Great Lakes Environmental Research Laboratory (GLERL).
